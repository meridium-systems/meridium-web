Product

Designed for connected operations across the enterprise.

MERIDIUM ERP brings together core workflows, master data, reporting and dashboards in one cloud-based business platform.

Core capability

Structured master data and transaction-driven workflows.

From stock items and supplier records to customer hierarchies and chart of accounts, the platform supports the core data foundation needed for reliable processing and reporting.

Stock Items

Create and maintain stock items with unique item codes, descriptions, categories and classifications. The product supports single and multiple units of measure, decimal precision, serial and batch controls, expiry dates, barcodes, supplier price details and min/max stock control by location.

✓
Flexible item setup

Use multiple UOM structures and quantity precision where needed.

✓
Warehouse visibility

Manage bin locations and stock controls at location level.

✓
Custom fields

Add organization-specific attributes without losing structure.

Creditors and Debtors

Maintain supplier and customer records before operational transactions begin. The product supports contact details, categories, payment methods, credit periods, transaction currency, postal addresses, delivery addresses, credit limits and parent-child customer structures.

✓
Supplier readiness

Support AP and purchasing flows with well-structured supplier data.

✓
Customer readiness

Enable quotes, sales orders, invoicing and receivables with complete customer masters.

✓
Foreign currency support

Manage cross-border trading relationships more efficiently.

General Ledger and finance structure

Create and maintain the chart of accounts with support for both main accounts and sub-accounts. Accounts can be managed as Balance Sheet or Profit & Loss and organized through account group, subgroup, type and subtype for stronger financial reporting and analysis.
